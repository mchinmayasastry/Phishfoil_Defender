About PhishGuard

PhishGuard is an AI-powered solution designed to protect users from phishing attacks and malicious websites. Our mission is to make the internet a safer place by providing accurate and reliable URL analysis.

Our Technology

We use a combination of machine learning algorithms, threat intelligence feeds, and real-time analysis to detect and prevent phishing attempts. Our system continuously learns and adapts to new threats as they emerge.

Stay Safe Online

  • Always verify the URL before entering sensitive information
  • Look for the padlock icon and "https://" in the address bar
  • Be cautious of unsolicited emails or messages with links
  • Keep your software and antivirus up to date
  • Use strong, unique passwords for different accounts
